Need Help?

  • What is a Queue ID and how do I get it

            A Queue ID is a unique, case-sensitive code given by the admin to join their queue. Make sure you enter it exactly as provided. You can join each queue only once.

  • What is buffer time?

             After you reach the front of the queue, buffer time is the period after which you are automatically moved to the end of the queue. We strongly recommend arriving 5-10 minutes before your turn.

  • What happens when it's my turn?

            When it's your turn, your status will update to show that it's your turn, and you'll receive an alert.

  • Can I join more than one queue?

             Absolutely. You can join multiple queues at once, making it easier to manage multiple appointments.

  • What happens if I leave a queue?

             Leaving a queue means you are removed permanently. Rejoining later will not guarantee the same position, so please leave carefully.
